About Us

Omnia Medical Services Co.

OMNIA MEDICAL SERVICES is one of CAMPS SERVICES SUDANESE CO., which is corporation of six companies (subsidiary of Sudastructure Company). Based on concept of health care, health environment and Medical products, the company has grown rapidly into a team aiming to contribute and fulfill costumer’s needs .

History

OMNIA MEDICAL AGENCIES. OMNIA was established in 1992, as one of the largest distributors of medicines, laboratory equipments and instruments, medical consumable and diagnostic reagents… In 2004, OMNIA MEDICAL SERVICES .CO. established a new department for health care and medical facilities, especially but not limited to the oil construction field as big investment in Sudan done now, and we have our own six medical facilities in different camps in southern of Sudan. In 2005 OMNIA MEDICAL SERVICES .CO. established to be sponsor and executive agent for many tenders and projects (e.g: Royal Care International Hospital).
